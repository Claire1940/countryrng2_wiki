If you want better event results, focus on the systems that improve the whole run instead of chasing one lucky moment. In Country RNG 2, the safest order is usually upgrade tree first, then luck, then roll speed, then resource boosts. That order keeps your account moving while still making rare hunts more efficient.

<Callout type="success" title="Best Priority Order">
Build your foundation first. Rare events are easier to enjoy when your core progression is already working.

| Priority | System | Why it matters | Best use case |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Upgrade tree | Builds long-term progress | Early and midgame sessions |
| 2 | Luck | Helps with rare-country hunting | Event-focused runs |
| 3 | Roll speed | Increases roll volume | Long sessions |
| 4 | Cash / Gold | Supports account growth | When upgrades slow down |

| Route | What you rely on | Strength | Weakness |
