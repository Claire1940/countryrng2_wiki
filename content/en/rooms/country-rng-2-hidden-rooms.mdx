<Section icon={Target} title="Search Routes and Scan Order">

The safest way to hunt Country RNG 2 hidden rooms is to use a fixed scan order. If you search randomly, you will miss more than you find. If you search the same pattern every time, you can compare results across servers and updates.

<Callout type="warning" title="Do Not Rush the Loop">
Do not sprint through the map and call every wall a secret. Hidden-room hunting works better when you slow down near geometry changes and re-check the same spot from multiple angles.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start at the most public area">
    Walk the main hub first. You want to learn the normal layout before you look for abnormal spaces. This makes later checks faster because you can spot what does not belong.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Move to edge zones">
    Inspect boundaries, corners, and any area where the map seems to stop too early. Hidden rooms often sit close to the edge because the layout needs a place to hide a shortcut, trigger, or decorative chamber.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check vertical layers">
    Look above and below your eye line. A room can be tucked under ramps, behind stacked props, or just over a ledge that you cannot see from normal standing height.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Test the route twice">
    Leave the area, re-enter it, and compare the result. If the space changes or becomes inaccessible after rejoining, log that detail instead of assuming the first result was wrong.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Write down the exact context">
    Note the server, time, and any active progression state you had. In a game with rolling, upgrades, and events, context can matter more than the first screenshot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Route Order | What You Look For | Best Use Case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Public hub first | Obvious seams, unused corners | Quick baseline check |
| Edge sweep second | Walls, ramps, dead ends | Most likely secret-entry zone |
| Vertical sweep third | Ceilings, underpasses, ledges | Finds hidden height differences |
| Re-entry test fourth | Persistent vs temporary spaces | Separates real rooms from glitches |

<Callout type="info" title="Route Logic">
